Episode 1: Voting Systems

The Electoral College has seen much controversy in recent years, but is it truly the root of our election system problems? What alternatives do we have? How have other countries attempted to set up their elections? Is there an ideal electoral system? In this episode, a group of students with diverse opinions discuss these questions and more.
